Government of Canada / Gouvernement du Canada

beta.Canada.ca

Government of Canada websites in development

As part of the Government of Canada’s commitment to experimentation, this web domain hosts websites and services that are being tested and validated with users. These “beta” products are still in development and are likely to change over time.

This domain collects ongoing “beta” products in one place, to make it easier for the government to collaborate and share its products in development. They are publicly available in the spirit of working in the open.

Get a beta.canada.ca subdomain

If you’re a Government of Canada team interested in hosting a product in development on beta.canada.ca, here’s what to do:

  1. Review the instructions and requirements
  2. Contact the Digital Transformation Office

This domain name is managed by the TBS Digital Transformation Office and the Canadian Digital Service. For technical information, visit the DNS repository on GitHub.

Websites and web services on beta.canada.ca are expected to meet Government of Canada standards for web content and service delivery but may use experimental design patterns or technology platforms.

What is a “beta” phase?

A “beta” phase involves building a working version of a website or service that can handle real transactions and work at scale. The product is still being developed, tested, and updated to reflect feedback from users.

Building products in the “beta” phase allows teams to:

  • improve the website or service based on feedback and performance data, on a larger scale
  • establish key performance indicators for the website or service
  • ensure that their website or service meets Government of Canada standards and requirements, including official languages, accessibility, design and brand, security and privacy
  • identify and resolve any barriers to using their website or service that might affect particular groups of people
  • determine if the solution should proceed to live production or full release status

A “beta” website or service that meets the Government of Canada Digital Standards can then become a “live” product on the Canada.ca domain.
